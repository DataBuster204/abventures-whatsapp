AB Ventures Hub - Customer Support Information

ABOUT US
AB Ventures Hub is a Nigerian business specialising in quality phones, computer gadgets, and real estate properties. We are committed to delivering the best products and properties at competitive prices.

PHONES AND GADGETS

Q: What brands of phones do you sell?
A: We sell a wide range of brands including Apple iPhone, Samsung, Tecno, Infinix, Xiaomi, and other quality brands. We stock both brand new and UK-used devices.

Q: Do you sell accessories?
A: Yes. We sell phone cases, screen protectors, chargers, earphones, power banks, laptop bags, and other accessories for all major brands.

Q: What computer products do you offer?
A: We sell laptops, desktop computers, monitors, keyboards, mice, routers, printers, and other computer peripherals. Brands include HP, Dell, Lenovo, Apple MacBook, and Asus.

Q: Are your products original?
A: Yes. All brand new products come with manufacturer warranty. UK-used devices are thoroughly inspected and graded before sale.

Q: Do you offer warranty?
A: Brand new phones and laptops come with a minimum of 12 months manufacturer warranty. UK-used devices come with a 3-month shop warranty from AB Ventures Hub.

Q: Can I pay in instalments?
A: Yes. We offer flexible payment plans for qualifying customers. Contact us directly to discuss instalment options for purchases above 150,000 Naira.

Q: Do you do repairs?
A: Yes. We offer repair services for phones and laptops including screen replacement, battery replacement, software issues, and general servicing.

Q: How do I place an order?
A: You can visit our store directly, send us a WhatsApp message, or call our customer service line. We also deliver within Lagos for an additional delivery fee.

Q: What are your delivery options?
A: We deliver within Lagos. Delivery fee depends on your location and is confirmed at the time of order. Delivery typically takes 24 to 48 hours.

Q: Can I return a product?
A: Yes. You can return a brand new product within 48 hours of purchase if it has a manufacturing defect. The product must be unused and in its original packaging. UK-used devices are not eligible for returns but are covered by the shop warranty.

REAL ESTATE

Q: What types of properties do you deal in?
A: We deal in residential land, residential houses, commercial properties, and short-let apartments across Lagos and select locations in Nigeria.

Q: Where are your properties located?
A: Our current listings include properties in Lekki, Ajah, Ibeju-Lekki, Epe, and other fast-growing areas of Lagos.

Q: Do you sell land?
A: Yes. We have dry land and waterfront land available in various sizes. All our land documents are verified and genuine.

Q: What documents come with your properties?
A: Our properties come with verified documents including Certificate of Occupancy (C of O), Deed of Assignment, Survey Plans, and Governor's Consent where applicable.

Q: Can I pay for land or property in instalments?
A: Yes. We offer flexible payment plans for land and property purchases. Initial deposit starts from 30 percent of the property value with the balance spread over an agreed period.

Q: Do you help with property inspection?
A: Yes. We arrange free property inspection visits for serious buyers. Contact us to schedule a visit.

Q: How do I know your properties are legitimate?
A: All our properties are verified by qualified legal professionals. We provide full documentation and can arrange for your own lawyer to verify before purchase.

Q: Do you offer property management services?
A: Yes. We can manage your property on your behalf including tenant sourcing, rent collection, and maintenance coordination.
